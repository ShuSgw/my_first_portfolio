Three Poster Series

Summary

The purpose of these pictures is to advertise fictional Karaoke event in Vancouver at a beach. I use illustrator and photoshop to design them. All posters are different from each other but they have the same concept which makes people want to join the Karaoke event. When I designed these posters I was considering how people can recognize that these posters are for the Karaoke event at first glace. Then, I decided to use images showing the big mic and shot of a beach in the background. Also, I used hands of various people because I would like to tell people that the event is for everyone comes from any country so that they can choose songs whatever they want to sing, such as songs from their countries.
