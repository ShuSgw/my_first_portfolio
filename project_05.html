News Letter Design

Summary

The concept is clickable and legible newsletter. For that concept, I choose flat design and pale colours because more than required 250 words are actually quite many and I need to adjust its design for them. Thus, i thought I should not use skeuomorph design which will be more complicated due to real images that tend to have many different colours and shades. Thus, what I create for this newsletters are all illustrations and flat design based.
